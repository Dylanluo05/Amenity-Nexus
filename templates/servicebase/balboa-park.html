General Description

Balboa Park is known for its gardens, trails and major attractions, including the San Diego Zoo and the colorful, tiled California Tower. Whether you want to see Dinosaurs at the San Diego Natural History Museum or view Shakespeare plays at the Old Globe Theatre Balboa Park is the place for a restful weekend getaway or day vacation.
