General Description

Khan Academy is a nonprofit organization with the mission of providing a free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere. Students learn at their own pace, going through video tutorials, practice questions, and sample test problems. This free resource covers all core subjects with the difficulty ranging from Kindergarten all the way to early college.
